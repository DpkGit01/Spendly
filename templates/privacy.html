Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. As you use Spendly, we store the expense records you add — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the features of Spendly — displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and enabling filters. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the Service. We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for keeping your login credentials confidential.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to render typefaces — this involves a request to Google's servers when you load a page. Beyond this, we do not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-sharing services. If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us. We are committed to addressing any concerns you may have about your privacy and will respond as promptly as possible.
